Aim and Goals



  • To standardize the process of managing bare metals in a kubernetes way and make processes like networking and storage simpler.

  • Making the management process portable in order to be used across different platforms and data centers.

  • Provide a user centric architecture that allows the user to choose what they need to satisfy their needs and requirements.

  • To make the whole procedure of deployment and management automated and reduce the time & effort required for doing it manually.

  • To enable the management of the kubernetes clusters in a declarative manner and supporting lifecycle management of the bare metal infrastructure.
